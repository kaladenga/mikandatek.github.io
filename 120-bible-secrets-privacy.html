Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

Thank you for using Bible Secrets ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

Information Collection and Use

Bible Secrets does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information.

The App does not require users to:

  • Create an account
  • Register or log in
  • Provide personal information
  • Submit personal content

Local Device Storage

The App stores only your selected language preference locally on your device using device storage technologies (such as Shared Preferences). This information never leaves your device and is used solely to remember your preferred language.

Advertisements

Bible Secrets does not display advertisements.

Analytics and Tracking

Bible Secrets does not use analytics services, tracking technologies, or behavioral advertising tools. The App does not monitor or track user activity.

Third-Party Services

Bible Secrets does not use third-party services that collect personal information from users.

Permissions

The App does not request sensitive device permissions and does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, or other personal device information.

Children's Privacy

Bible Secrets is intended for a general audience, including children. Since the App does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Since no personal information is collected, transmitted, or stored by the App, there are no personal data records maintained by us.
